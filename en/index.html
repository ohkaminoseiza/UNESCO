Who Is AI For? Reading UNESCO's Recommendation on the Ethics of Artificial Intelligence

April 5, 2026 | AI, Education

In 2021, UNESCO adopted the Recommendation on the Ethics of Artificial Intelligence — the first international normative framework on AI agreed upon by 193 countries. This recommendation goes beyond asking "how should we use AI?" to ask something deeper: "what kind of society do we want to build?"
