Ethical Challenges and Possibilities of Generative AI in Education

April 3, 2026 | AI, Education

While introducing Generative AI in educational settings brings many benefits, it also requires deep discussions regarding copyrights, privacy, and the cultivation of learners' thinking skills.

1. The Possibilities Offered by Generative AI

The potential of Generative AI is immense — from enabling personalized learning to reducing the workload of teachers. It is also highly effective as a thinking partner that supports students' inquiry-based learning.

2. Ethical Challenges We Face

However, many concerns have been raised, including the accuracy of responses (the problem of hallucination) and the risk of outsourcing thinking entirely to AI, which may hinder learners' creativity.

3. The Approach Required for Future Education

Rather than treating AI as a "magic box that gives the right answers," there is an urgent need for a new kind of information literacy education that positions AI as a "partner for deepening thinking."
